WHAT IS SUNIB HEALTH?

       SUNIB HEALTH is one of the organizations at BINUS University that contributes to 
 the health sector. SUNIB HEALTH was established in 2022, the same year the SUNIB Fo- 
 od Court opened. SUNIB HEALTH's main goal is to ensure that everyone is guaranteed 
 health on a universal scale. In addition, SUNIB HEALTH also organizes innovative health 
 events and helps in developing social care for one another as well as providing articles 
 related to physical health.

EVENT

Sunib Health Event is a certain activity or festival that shows, displays and celebrates to commemorate important things that are held at a certain time.
